Vikingland

Viking33000×3000 495 KB

Vikingland is a NFT marketplace initially started as a side project to rewards stakers on www.stakenordic.com. It’s now being developed on it’s own and currently has automatic orders in place. Future features will be profiles for selling/creating users own NFTs. NFTs are marked with the wallet address of the buyer and minting of the NFTs will be done when Babylon is released and it’s technically possible. Payment in the marketplace acceps $XRD and $VKC. $VKC is the VikingCoin being airdropped for stakers on www.stakenordic.com

The NFT collection currently live is the “Viking Warrior”, but more NFTs will be produced. Eventually users can add and sell their own.

Price of each NFT: 15 XRD
