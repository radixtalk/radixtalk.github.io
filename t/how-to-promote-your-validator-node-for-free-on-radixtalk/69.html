If you are a member of the Registered Validator group then you can promote your Validator node with a free text advert on RadixTalk.

How does it work?

  1. Join the Registered Validator group
How to join the Registered Validators group Validators

  1. Write a new topic in #staking:validator-introductions to introduce your Validator Node.

  2. Send a message to @Stuart with the proposed content of your advert. The ad will be linked to your Validator Introduction post in #staking:validator-introductions

  3. Your text advert will be added to the list of house adverts that will be cycled through in order starting with a random one.
