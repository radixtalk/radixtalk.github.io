The amount of projects on Radix grows rapidly. A good place to start exploring these projects, is Radix List. It attempts to collect and categorize all projects on Radix.

Some noteworthy projects are:

  • OciSwap, a decentralized exchange which is scheduled to launch on day 1 of Babylon. Similarly, DefiPlaza and CERBY will also be launching DEX initiatives on Radix.
  • Doge³, the first memecoin on Radix, which will be listed on VinDAX as of 7 January, 2022. There are plenty other memecoins as well, such as Samusky and DIX.
  • CerbyMask, a browser wallet
  • Nerds Republic, the first NFT project. Other NFT projects include Abandoned Scorpions and HOARD.
