Russell from Radix provided a comprehensive explanation as follows:

  • Stake on nodes is not stored as an amount of XRD, but a number of StakeUnits.
  • The XRD amount corresponding to a 1 StakeUnit differs from node to node. Presently, all nodes start their life at 1 XRD per 1 StakeUnit, but network emissions will adjust this number over time.
