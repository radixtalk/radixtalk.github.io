Why don't Validators outside the Top 100 show any Recent Uptime?

Questions / Help
uptime, faq, validators
Stuart (Stuart | RadixPool) 26 December 2021 20:33 #1

The Recent Uptime indication of a Validator shows the percentage of “proposals” the validator has successfully made over the past 500 epochs (roughly 2 weeks).

The record of completed proposals and missed proposals is saved on the Radix ledger - but only for the Top 100 validators. Since there is no record of completed or missed proposals for validator nodes outside the Top 100, it is not possible to show a Recent Uptime indication for these nodes.
