Radical Staking Running a Giveaway from 02/21/2022 to 03/22/2022

Staking
RadicalStaking (Wilson) 21 February 2022 13:39 #1

Radical Staking is running a giveaway for both new and existing delegators from 02/21 to 03/22 that includes $XRD, $OCI, $DGC, Radix Collection, Radical Penguins, and Abandoned Scorpions–including highly ranked rare scorps!

Any new delegation of at least 1,000 XRD (or increasing your delegation by at least 1,000 XRD, if you’re already a Radical Staking delegator) from 02/21 to 03/22 will enter your wallet address into a random draw for the following prizes:

1st place: 2,000 XRD + 1,000 OCI + 1,000,000 DGC (DogeCube) + Abandoned Scorpion #3114 (518th out of 10,000 total rarity!) + 1 Radical Penguin

2nd place: 1,000 XRD + 500 OCI + 500,000 DGC + Abandoned Scorpion #6633 (562nd out of 10,000 total rarity!) + 1 Radical Penguin

3rd place: 500 XRD + 250 OCI + 250,000 DGC + Abandoned Scorpion #7156 (1,258th out of 10,000 total rarity!) + 1 Radical Penguin

But wait, there’s more If you follow @RadicalStaking on Twitter, and like and retweet this tweet you will also be entered to win an additional prize of 2,000 XRD + 2,000 RADC (Radix Collection token): https://twitter.com/RadicalStaking/status/1495740601807122436. This is a separate random draw on top of the three prizes described above, so you can win even more!

HOW TO PARTICIPATE: Simply delegate 1,000 XRD or more to Radical Staking using our validator address, rv1qwqf39p307f58y06ejewfwzyuyqd68denpdk3vpp7rsvmqtsc0kdkhq64gc, or increase your current delegation by at least 1,000 XRD if you’re already a current delegator, from 02/21 to 03/22.

The Radical Staking team is really excited to conduct our first giveaway for the Radix community and we hope everyone participates! We really appreciate all the support from the community since the Radix mainnet went live, and we wanted to take this opportunity to give back to the community and also support some of our favorite Radix projects
